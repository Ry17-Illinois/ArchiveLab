# Editathon User Guide

## Getting Started

1. **Login** with your username and password
2. You'll see your assigned pages in the left sidebar
3. Click any page to start working on it

## Working on a Page

### 1. Review the Document Image
- The original document image appears on the left
- Use the zoom controls (+/-/Reset) to see details

### 2. Validate Metadata
At the top, you'll see document metadata (title, date, creator, etc.)
- Click **✓** to approve correct information
- Click **✗** to reject incorrect information  
- Click **×** to remove irrelevant information

### 3. Validate Named Entities
Below metadata, you'll see extracted names, places, organizations, etc.
- Hover over any entity to see approve/reject buttons
- Click **✓** to approve
- Click **✗** to reject

### 4. Create Your Transcription

**Choose a starting point:**
1. Review the OCR versions (tabs like "Tesseract", "OpenAI", "Claude")
2. Click the version that looks most accurate
3. Click **"Use as Base & Edit"**

**Edit the transcription:**
- The text becomes editable
- Fix any errors you see
- Compare with the document image on the left
- Your edits create a "Human-Reviewed" version

### 5. Save Your Work
- Click **"Save & Continue"** when done
- This saves everything and moves to the next page
- Your progress is tracked automatically

## Navigation

- **Previous/Next buttons** - Move between pages
- **Page list (left sidebar)** - Jump to any page
  - ○ = Not started
  - ⋯ = In progress  
  - ✓ = Completed

## Tips

- You can switch between OCR versions anytime to compare
- The "Human-Reviewed" tab shows your edited version
- Zoom in to read difficult handwriting or small text
- Take your time - accuracy matters more than speed

## Need Help?

Contact your editathon administrator if you have questions or encounter issues.
